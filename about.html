Overview

Hi-Tech Metal Formings Established in 1996, Hi-Tech Metal Formings Indore(M.P) is a premium quality manufacturer & supplier of Sheet Metal Parts, Fabricated Assemblies, Air Reservoirs, Mechanical Jacks & Fire Extinguishers. Manufacturing facility is certified to IATF 16949:2016 Quality Systems & ISO 14001:2015 Environmental Management system. We give utmost importance to quality and providing Optimum products to suit your needs. With excellent Infrastructure, extensive manufacturing facilities, dedicated young team we strive to serve our customers with Quality products, Timely deliveries with Competent Price.

We are well known for providing tailor made solutions with international standards to meet the specified customer requirements through our dedicated team of technical professionals. These are the primary reasons why our customers rely on us regarding Quality , Cost Efficiency , Reliability & Timely deliveries. For our achievements , we have been honored with some prestigious awards in new product development and supply chain management.
