Hi-Tech Metal Formings, headquartered in Indore, India, is a leading international supplier of equipment and services to the international markets.

2016

In 2016, Hi-Tech Metal Formings was founded in Indore, India. The company started its operations with a vision to provide high-quality metal forming solutions to industries worldwide.

Within its first year, Hi-Tech Metal Formings made significant strides in research and development, introducing innovative metal forming techniques that set new industry standards. The company quickly gained recognition for its commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction.

Today, Hi-Tech Metal Formings continues to thrive as a global leader in metal forming technology, serving a diverse range of industries with cutting-edge solutions.

2018

In 2018, Hi-Tech Metal Formings expanded its production facilities, increasing its capacity to meet growing market demands. The company's dedication to technological advancements and quality assurance continued to set industry benchmarks.

During this year, Hi-Tech Metal Formings also achieved recognition for its sustainability initiatives, implementing eco-friendly practices in its manufacturing processes.

2020

In 2020, Hi-Tech Metal Formings introduced cutting-edge automation technologies into its production processes, enhancing efficiency and precision. This marked a significant milestone in the company's journey towards Industry 4.0.

Despite global challenges, Hi-Tech Metal Formings remained committed to its customers, providing uninterrupted support and solutions during these times.

2022

In 2022, Hi-Tech Metal Formings celebrated its continued growth and global presence. The company expanded into new international markets, establishing partnerships with industry leaders and further solidifying its reputation as a reliable supplier.

With a focus on innovation and sustainability, Hi-Tech Metal Formings remained at the forefront of the metal forming industry, delivering high-quality solutions to meet evolving market needs.
